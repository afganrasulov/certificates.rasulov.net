Renowned Educator Endorsed by Influencers

Afgan Rasulov, with over a decade of experience in the digital realm, stands as a testament to dedication and innovation. His journey commenced in 2013 when Ruhi Çenet, then with just 30,000 followers, recognized the potential in Rasulov’s ideas and collaborated with him. Their professional relationship flourished, evolving into a close family friendship over the years.

During the early days, Rasulov meticulously conducted research on YouTube content strategies in both Russian and English, reading extensively and sharing his insights regularly with Çenet. The implementation of these ideas contributed significantly to the growth of Çenet's channel, attracting the attention of numerous other YouTubers eager to collaborate with Rasulov.

Today, Rasulov has had the privilege of working with many YouTubers who have achieved millions of followers. Witnessing the application of his advice and strategies on their channels brings him immense satisfaction.

For over a decade, Rasulov has thrived in the digital world. Currently, he serves as the Digital Marketing Director at Acun Medya Akademi, providing innovative digital solutions to the academy. Additionally, he is a part of the expert teaching staff specializing in AI-driven digital content production at Acun Medya Akademi.

Furthermore, Rasulov has created 17 courses and boasts an impressive 300,000 subscribers on YouTube. His extensive experience and contributions to the field have established him as a highly sought-after educator and consultant in the digital content creation industry.
